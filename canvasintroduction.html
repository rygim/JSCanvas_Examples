@(:title="Canvas Introduction") @(:header="Quick Canvas Introduction")

The canvas is accessed through javascript

var ctx = document.getElementById("application-canvas")
                           .getContext("2d");

Interacting with the canvas: 

ctx.drawImage(imageObj, x, y)
 
ctx.fillText(text, x, y)
 
ctx.arc(x, y, radius, 
    startRadians, endRadians, isAnticlockwise)
 
ctx.fillRectangle(x, y, width, height)

Way more interesting, less demo-able methods

  • ctx.getImageData(x, y, width, height)
  • ctx.toDataUrl()
